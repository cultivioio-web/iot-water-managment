Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: December 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cultivio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AquaSense products and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Information

  • Device identifiers (MAC address, serial number)
  • Device configuration settings
  • Water level readings and pump status
  • Device health and connectivity status

2.2 Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (when you contact us)
  • Email address (for warranty registration)
  • Phone number (for support and delivery)
  • Shipping address (for product delivery)

2.3 Usage Data

  • App usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Feature preferences
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Product Functionality: To provide and maintain our water management services
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Product Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our products
  • Communication: To send you important updates, warranty information, and (with consent) promotional materials
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Processing: Most data is processed locally on your devices via Zigbee mesh network
  • No Cloud Dependency: Core functionality works without internet connection
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption

5. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in delivering our services (shipping, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Essential functionality (session management)
  • Analytics (anonymous usage statistics)
  • Preferences (remembering your settings)

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
