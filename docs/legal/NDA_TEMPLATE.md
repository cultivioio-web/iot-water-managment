## RECITALS

**WHEREAS** the Discloser is engaged in the business of developing and selling smart water management devices under the brand "Cultivio" and "AquaSense";

**WHEREAS** the Recipient desires to receive certain confidential information from the Discloser for the purpose of _________________________________ (hereinafter referred to as the "**Purpose**");

**WHEREAS** the Discloser is willing to disclose such confidential information subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement;

**NOW THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

1.1 "**Confidential Information**" means any and all information disclosed by the Discloser to the Recipient, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to:

   (a) Technical information: designs, drawings, schematics, specifications, firmware source code, algorithms, PCB layouts, component selections, manufacturing processes;
   
   (b) Business information: customer lists, supplier details, pricing, cost structures, marketing strategies, financial projections;
   
   (c) Product information: product roadmaps, unreleased features, development plans;
   
   (d) Trade secrets: proprietary processes, formulas, techniques;
   
   (e) Any other information designated as "Confidential" at the time of disclosure.

1.2 Confidential Information does **NOT** include information that:

   (a) Was publicly known at the time of disclosure;
   (b) Becomes publicly known through no fault of the Recipient;
   (c) Was rightfully in the Recipient's possession prior to disclosure;
   (d) Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of Confidential Information;
   (e) Is disclosed with the prior written consent of the Discloser.

---

## 2. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT

2.1 The Recipient agrees to:

   (a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;
   
   (b) Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose stated above;
   
   (c) Not disclose, publish, or otherwise reveal any Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
   
   (d) Take all reasonable measures to protect the secrecy of the Confidential Information, using at least the same degree of care as used to protect its own confidential information;
   
   (e) Limit access to Confidential Information to those employees, agents, or contractors who have a need to know and who are bound by similar confidentiality obligations;
   
   (f) Not copy or reproduce the Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose.

2.2 The Recipient shall be liable for any breach of this Agreement by its employees, agents, or contractors.

---

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of the Discloser.

3.2 This Agreement does not grant the Recipient any license, right, or interest in any trademark, copyright, patent, or other intellectual property of the Discloser.

3.3 Any improvements, modifications, or derivative works created using the Confidential Information shall be the sole property of the Discloser.

---

## 4. NON-COMPETE (Optional)

4.1 During the term of this Agreement and for a period of _____ (______) months thereafter, the Recipient shall not, directly or indirectly:

   (a) Engage in any business that competes with the Discloser's smart water management products;
   
   (b) Solicit or attempt to solicit any customer or supplier of the Discloser;
   
   (c) Hire or attempt to hire any employee of the Discloser.

---

## 5. RETURN OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

5.1 Upon termination of this Agreement or upon request by the Discloser, the Recipient shall:

   (a) Immediately cease using the Confidential Information;
   
   (b) Return or destroy all copies of the Confidential Information (written, electronic, or otherwise);
   
   (c) Provide written certification of such return or destruction within seven (7) days.

---

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

6.1 This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and continue for a period of _____ (______) years, unless terminated earlier by either Party with thirty (30) days written notice.

6.2 The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination and continue for a period of _____ (______) years from the date of termination.

---

## 7. REMEDIES

7.1 The Recipient acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Discloser for which monetary damages may be inadequate.

7.2 The Discloser shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, and any other remedies available at law or in equity.

7.3 The Recipient agrees to pay all costs, including reasonable attorney's fees, incurred by the Discloser in enforcing this Agreement.

---

## 8. GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 **Governing Law**: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

8.2 **Jurisdiction**: Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in _____________, India.

8.3 **Entire Agreement**: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.

8.4 **Amendment**: This Agreement may not be amended except in writing signed by both Parties.

8.5 **Severability**: If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

8.6 **Waiver**: Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver of future enforcement.

8.7 **Assignment**: This Agreement may not be assigned by the Recipient without prior written consent of the Discloser.
